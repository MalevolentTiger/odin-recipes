Breakfast Burrito

Description

This is a recipe that I have made multiple times and enjoy every time. It's simple and takes a couple of hours in the crockpot.

Ingredients

    Breakfast Bowl
    Tortilla
    Shredded Cheese

Steps

  • Step 1: Make the breakfast bowl in the microwave.
  • Step 2: Split the breakfast bowl evenly into two tortillas and add some extra shredded cheese.
  • Step 3: Heat a pan up and lay the burritos in it for a minute or two of both sides to slightly brown them and help them not open back up.
  • Step 4: You're done! Repeat for as many as you want to make. Can easily wrap in aluminium foil and freeze for later.
